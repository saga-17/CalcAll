Basic Calculator

Perform fundamental arithmetic operations with ease.

Step 1: Enter your first number in the 'A value' field.
Step 2: Enter your second number in the 'B value' field.
Step 3: Choose your desired operation from the dropdown menu.
Step 4: Click the 'Calculate' button to see your result.
The calculator supports Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, Power, and Modulo operations.

Simple Interest Calculator

Calculate simple interest for loans and investments.

Step 1: Enter your Principal Amount (the initial investment or loan amount) in the first field.
Step 2: Enter the Time Period in years.
Step 3: Enter the Interest Rate as a percentage.
Step 4: Click 'Calculate' to see your interest amount.
The result will show you the total interest earned or paid over the specified period.

Compound Interest Calculator

Advanced interest calculation with compounding periods.

Step 1: Enter your Principal Amount (initial investment) in the first field.
Step 2: Enter the Time Period in years.
Step 3: Enter the Annual Interest Rate as a percentage.
Step 4: Specify how many times the interest is compounded per year.
Step 5: Click 'Calculate' to see your compound interest.
Remember, more frequent compounding periods will result in higher returns on your investment.
